## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how `Johnny Collado` ("we," "us," or "our") handles
information in connection with the Playfield Portal application (the "App"). It should be
read together with our [Terms of Service](terms-of-service.md).

We designed the App to keep your data on your own device wherever possible. We do not sell
your personal information.

## 2. Who We Are

The App is provided by `Johnny Collado`. For any privacy question or request, contact
us at `NEXTJJEN@gmail.com`.

## 3. Information We Collect

**a. Data stored on your device.** The App organizes and launches content you already own
or have installed. Information such as your library configuration, file and folder paths,
game and media metadata, artwork, and app settings is stored **locally on your device**. We
do not collect or receive this information on our own servers.

**b. Discord account data (only if you connect Discord).** The Social section is optional.
If you choose to connect a Discord account, you authorize the App to access certain
information from Discord through the Discord Social SDK, which may include:

- Your Discord identity (user ID, username, and avatar);
- Your friends / relationships list and their online status;
- Presence and activity status (both the activity the App broadcasts on your behalf and the
  activity of your friends that the App displays to you);
- Connection and session information needed to establish voice chat.

This data is processed to provide the Social features and is exchanged with Discord under
Discord's own Privacy Policy.

**c. Voice communications.** When you use voice chat, audio is transmitted in real time
through Discord's infrastructure to the other participants. **The App does not record,
store, or retain your voice audio.**

**d. Device permissions.** The App may request the following permissions, each used only for
its stated purpose:

- **Microphone** — to transmit your voice during voice chat;
- **Storage / file access** — to locate and launch the games and media you configure;
- **Network access** — to communicate with Discord for the Social features.

**e. Diagnostic logs.** The App may write diagnostic logs locally on your device to help
you troubleshoot. These logs stay on your device unless you choose to share them with us.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information solely to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the App's core features;
- Enable the optional Discord Social features you turn on (activity status, friends, voice);
- Respond to your support requests;
- Protect the App and its users and comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

## 5. How Information Is Shared

- **With Discord.** If you use the Social features, information described in Section 3(b)
  and 3(c) is shared with Discord to deliver those features. Discord's handling of that data
  is governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.
- **With third-party apps you launch.** When you launch an emulator, game, or other app
  through the App, that separate application operates under its own privacy practices, which
  we do not control.
- **For legal reasons.** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the
  rights, safety, or property of our users or us.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Discord Inc.

## 6. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device remains until you delete it, clear the App's data, or
uninstall the App. Discord account data accessed through the Social SDK is retained only for
as long as your Discord account is connected; disconnecting your Discord account or
uninstalling the App ends the App's access.

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of
electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute
security.

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

- **Discord connection.** Connecting Discord is optional; you can disconnect it at any time
  from within the App or by revoking the App's access in your Discord account settings.
- **Permissions.** You can grant or revoke device permissions (such as microphone) through
  your device settings; some features may not work without them.
- **Local data.** You can clear the App's stored data or uninstall the App at any time.
- **Legal rights.** Depending on your location (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA), you may
  have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. To exercise any such right,
  contact us at `NEXTJJEN@gmail.com`.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your
jurisdiction, if higher). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If
you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us at `NEXTJJEN@gmail.com`.

## 10. International Data Transfers

If you use the Discord integration, your information may be processed by Discord in countries
other than your own. By using the Social features, you acknowledge that such transfers are
governed by Discord's Privacy Policy.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will
update the "Last updated" date and, where practicable, provide notice within the App. Your
continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised
policy.

## 12. Contact

Questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy may be sent to `NEXTJJEN@gmail.com`.
